Acid Reflux/GRED

Symptoms

Acidity
Chest pain
Difficulty swallowing
Heart Burn
Nausea

Home Remedies

1. Keep a food journal and avoid trigger foods
2. Resist the urge to overeat or eat quickly
3. Avoid late meals, snacking before bed and eating before exercising
4. Eat alkaline foods, like a ripe banana, drinking cold milk
5. Reduce stress

Medications

Allopathy : Oral pantoprazole, Pepcid, Nexium.
Ayurvedic : Kamdudha Ras,Amlant
